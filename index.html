Jacob Glover

Cofounder and President

I'm Jacob Glover, a junior at Langley High School. In addition to preserving the environment, I love to swim, play the clarinet, and learn about history. I am proud of the work we have managed to do so far conserving Lake Fairfax Park. As we continue to grow and gain new volunteers, I am excited for us to expand to more parks and make an even bigger difference in our community!
